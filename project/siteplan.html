Site Plan for Expenses and Budget Tracker

Expenses and Budget Tracker

Expenses and Budget Tracker - I chose this name because it clearly shows exactly what the website does: provides you a way to track your expenses and budget

Optional domain: expensestracker.com

Site Purpose

This site will serve as a expense and budget tracker. You will be able to create a budget, track expenses, see charts based on your incomings and expenses and plan ahead what you'll do with your money. This will help you to use your money wisely

Scenarios

  • What tool can help me track my expenses?
  • How can I manage my money better?
  • Can I visualize in a chart my expenses and incomings?

Color Schema

Theme color: #34a853 for headers and navigation

Text color: #ffffff for titles and important text information

Complementary color: #b4b4b4 for other texts in the websites

Dark background: #0e0e11 for the website background

Typography
